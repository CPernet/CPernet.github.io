Home

Welcome on my personal personal website.

I keep here things which are of interest for my research and might be of interest to others; in particular guidelines for writing fMRI articles, information about fMRI designs, the BOLD signal, neurophysiology (neural histology, communication, relation to the vasular system and neuro-glia) and my favorite, statistics.

I also write a lot of code in Matlab, for statistics, fMRI and EEG with several projects of toolboxes etc. Not everything is open access yet, but it's getting there .. check also my download page.

Some of these codes find there way into lectures, some of them might of interest for junior neuro-imagers and neuro-scientists.
